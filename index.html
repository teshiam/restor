Our story

A return to yourself.

Restor began with a simple realization: we spend so much of our lives pushing through that we forget what it feels like to slow down.

What started as one woman's search for a gentler way to care for herself became something bigger
a belief that wellness isn't about fixing yourself. It's about making space to listen, to care with intention,
and to return to yourself, one day at a time.

Because your body remembers.

It remembers the weight you carry, the pace you've kept, and the moments you've chosen yourself.
That's why we create thoughtful care that supports your body with the gentleness it deserves.

Castor oil is where our story begins, not where it ends. Every product we create is designed to
help you slow down, reconnect, and care for yourself in ways that last.
